What is Jackbox Games?

Jackbox Games is a studio that creates exciting party games, known for hits like Quiplash, Fibbage, Drawful, Trivia Murder Party and many more.

Our goal is to bring people together for fun, quirky, and unforgettable experiences.

What is a Party Pack?

Each Party Pack includes five fun-filled games that can be played by groups of any size.

We’ve created several packs over the years, each offering a unique mix of drawing, trivia, writing, and identity games that guarantee fun for all players—whether . in person or online.

I’ve Bought a Party Pack! What Should My Friends or Family Do to Join?

Once you’ve purchased a Party Pack, your friends and family don’t need to buy anything! Simply share the host screen with them, and they can join in on their phones, tablets, or computers. It’s that easy to get the party started!

How Do I Start a Jackbox Game?

To start your game, simply open the Party Pack you purchased, select a game from the list, and follow the prompts. Once the game is live, share the unique game code with your friends so they can join in and play from their devices.

The key is making sure everyone in your group (either in person or remotely) can see and hear the screen running the game. This will allow them to see the room code and join, and to see what’s happening in the game and play along.

What Should I Do if I Encounter Problems While Playing?

If you run into any issues while playing, try refreshing the page, checking your internet connection, or restarting the game.

If the issue persists, visit our support page or contact our team for further assistance. We’re here to help you keep the fun going!
